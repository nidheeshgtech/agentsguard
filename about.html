About Us

We Secure the Agentic Internet.

Founded by security practitioners, cryptographers and engineers, AgentsGuard closes the gap traditional tooling was never designed to solve. AgentsGuard builds the runtime enforcement layer that makes autonomous AI trustworthy at enterprise scale.

Our Mission

Built on the Belief That AI Must Be Governed.

We started AgentsGuard as the industry embraces agentic AI, deploying it at scale, without solving the core security problem.

Autonomous agents with access to critical systems can erase content, leak sensitive data, and compromise operations without reliable enforcement layers.

Our team comes from security research and distributed systems, where we saw the same failure patterns in highly scalable configurations that created major security issues.

AgentsGuard exists to build the secure framework every organization needs when deploying autonomous systems using agentic AI.

Enforcement-First

Security is never optional. Every agent action passes through a control point.

Radical Transparency

We publish threat models, share research openly, and design for auditability.

The Team

Security Engineers Who Refused to Look Away.

Our team is drawn from security research, distributed systems, and applied AI.
